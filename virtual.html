Virtual Events

The challenges of gathering during the pandemic are at the intersection of movement and computing. Thus, MOCO aims to be a pioneer in rethinking conference attendence.

In 2021 we will organize a virtual event that aims to be a (temporal) explosion of the conference as a site of knowledge production. Learning from this event as well as 2020's all-virtual conference, we then intend to host both in-person and virtual events in 2022.
